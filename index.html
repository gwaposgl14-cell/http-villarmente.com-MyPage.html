About & History

Bais City National Science High School (BCNSHS) was established to provide an advanced science and mathematics curriculum to selected learners in Bais City and neighboring communities. Over the years the school has developed a culture of research, competitions, and environmental stewardship while remaining grounded in Filipino values.

Mission (School)

To provide a nurturing and challenging environment that fosters scientific inquiry, critical thinking, and values formation to produce competent and responsible citizens.

Vision (School)

To be a leading institution of science and technology education in the region — producing graduates who excel in STEM and serve their communities with integrity.

DepEd Mission & Vision

Mission: To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment and teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.

Vision: We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

Academic Programs

Junior High School (Grades 7–10)

Enriched curriculum in science & mathematics, laboratory work, ICT literacy, introductory research skills, and co-curricular activities to develop strong academic foundations.

Senior High School (Grades 11–12) — STEM Strand

The Senior High Academic Track (STEM) focuses on advanced science and mathematics, engineering principles, research methods, and industry-relevant skills needed for careers in science, engineering, computing, and health sciences.

Research & Extension

Students undertake guided research projects, mentorship with teachers, and participate in division/regional science fairs and community outreach projects.

Co-Curricular & Clubs

Robotics Club, Science Club, Mathletes, Environmental Brigade, Student Government — these develop soft skills and leadership outside the classroom.

What is STEM & Why it matters

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It is an educational approach that integrates these four disciplines to develop problem-solving, analytical thinking, innovation, and practical skills. Importance: STEM equips students with critical thinking, computational literacy, laboratory skills, and research abilities that are in high demand for 21st-century careers — from healthcare and engineering to data science and environmental research.

STEM Program Features (sample activities)

  • Laboratory experiments & inquiry-based learning
  • Student-led research & capstone projects
  • Robotics and coding workshops
  • Fieldwork (environmental monitoring, community science)
  • Industry/college partnerships & guest lectures
